Tourism in Australia

Tourism is one of the most important drivers of the Australian economy, attracting millions of domestic and international visitors every year. This dashboard provides an overview of tourism activity across Australia, highlighting trends in travel, popular destinations, and the purposes of trips.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Number of trips per city (1998 - 2016)

This visualization shows the distribution of trips by city across Australia, each circle represents a city, and the size of the circle corresponds to the total number of trips. Larger circles indicate cities that attract a higher volume of tourists, such as Sydney and Melbourne, while smaller circles highlight destinations with more limited but still notable tourism activity.

International arrivals and departures

On this visualization we can compare the international arrivals and departures from October 2015 to April 2025. The blue line represents arrivals, while the orange line represents departures.

For most of the period before 2020, arrivals and departures followed a similar seasonal pattern, with small fluctuations of travelers per month. Then in 2020 a big drop occured, reflecting the impact of the Covid 19 pandemic and border closures. Travel remained near zero for over a year, before gradually recovering from 2021 onwards. By 2023, both arrivals and departures showed strong growth, reaching levels similar to the pre pandemic period

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Purpose of the trip

Understanding why people travel to Australia is essential for analyzing the tourism industry. Different purposes of travel such as holidays, visiting relatives, or business, highlight the diverse roles that tourism plays in the economy and society. Looking at the breakdown of trip purposes helps identify which segments are the most significant and how they contribute to overall travel demand.
